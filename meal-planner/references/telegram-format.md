# Telegram Formatting Guidance

Use compact formatting for reminders.

Preferred pattern:

```text
【今日菜谱｜周X】
餐次 | 内容 | 备注
早餐 | `...` | `...`
午餐 | `...` | `...`
晚餐 | `...` | `...`

【本周主采购】 / 【今日补货】
类别 | 食材
...

【备注】
- `...`
- `...`
```

Fallback to short lists if the table becomes cramped.

Keep the reminder within roughly one to two mobile screens when possible.
